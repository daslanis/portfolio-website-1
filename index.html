About me:


Greetings everyone! My name is Zack and I'm a character designer and 2D artist from Egypt.

I specialize in anime art styles, more specifically character designs and action scenes. I can provide character sheets and designs for clothes, armor and weapons. Furthermore, I can provide posters, comics and video game assets, I'll do just about anything regarding characters.

I will not do NSFW art or anything that includes nudity, LGBTQ for religious reasons and anything regarding scenery or complex backgrounds and land scape, other than that I'm your go to guy!
